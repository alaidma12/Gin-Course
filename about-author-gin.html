A note on the inspiration
This course began with a person, not a brief. Amber's partner has a genuine love of gin — the kind that comes with strong opinions about tonic, an opinion on every back bar, and the ability to make a G&T feel like an occasion. Learning to talk about gin the way he does became the brief. The course is the result.
